Types of Burritos

There are many kinds of Burritos. Burritos come in all shapes and sizes! Burrito's are often meats, beans, and cheeses along with spices and sometimes rice. Burrito's are the Mexican version of the Sandwich!

The Cheese and Bean Burrito is the most common of Burritos. It is beloved by simpletons who can't handle a real Burrito. It is the "Grilled Cheese" of the Burritoverse.

Then there is the all powerful and LEGENDARY Breakfast Burrito The Breakfast Burrito is for the people who wake up in the early morning and are craving something delicious. Breakfast Burritos come packed with things like sausage, bacon, eggs, salsa and other goods. It is known as the "Italian Hoagie" of the Burritoverse.

There of course are other Burrito's out there to satisfy the l ikes of many. Mexican Restaurants present you with things like the Smothered Burrito and Taco Time presented us with the all famous Crispy Burrito
